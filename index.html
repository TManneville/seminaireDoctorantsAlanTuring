Seminary of PhD students of Alan Turing building

This seminary gathers PhD students of Alan Turing building: those of the LIX and of The INRIA Saclay. It is open to everybody.

There is a session more or less every two weeks. The dates and schedules are decided during free seminary weeks via a foodle fulfilled by PhD students. The foodle is closed on the Thursday before the week of the session.

Calendar of the sessions

  • 11th session : 01/10/2015 at 11.am. in room Flajolet Louis Dumont Reciprocity laws and their rational variants
    Abstract: I invite you to an adventure in the realm of reciprocity laws. If you have never come across them, you will discover these fascinating creatures. If you have already seen one before, you will learn how to tame them. Our weapons: the wisdom of the ancient algebraic number theorists, algorithms and computers. Everyone will come back home knowing how to answer questions such as the following: An integer x and a prime p are given. Is it possible to find an integer y such that x=y^3 modulo p ?
